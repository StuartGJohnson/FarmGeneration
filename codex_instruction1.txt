The arguments for generation of orchard_world.usda are below. For now we will generate a orchard of n_rows rows with n_cols trees in each row. Later we will make this more interesting. Arguments to our generator are:

n_rows (default 2)
n_cols (default 2)
tree_scaling_min (default 0.8)
tree_scaling_max (default 1.2)
ground_cover_scaling_min (default 1.0)
ground_cover_scaling_max (default 1.0)
ground_extent (meters) (default 2)
row_spacing (meters) (default 4)
col_spacing (meters) (default 3)

Note the ground cover assets come in 1m x 1m patches - they should cover a ground plane which extends between all the trees and also out to an extent ground_extent meters beyond where the tree assets are placed. Note all assets should be uniformly-randomly scaled by the associated scaling, and also rotated randomly about the z (up) axis. Note that the ground cover assets should be rotated in random increments of 90 degress (they are square).

The assets should have visual, rigid body and collider settings as follows:

GroundPlane:
visual off
collider on
rigid_body off/static

ground cover:
visual on
collider off
rigid_body off

trees:
visual on
collider off
rigid_body off
