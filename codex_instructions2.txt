Generate a large ground mesh with collider and UVs, then create a tileable procedural dirt albedo texture PNG and bind it to the ground material.

Requirements:
- One ground mesh, no repeated dirt tiles.
- Ground mesh has UV coordinates.
- Material uses UsdPreviewSurface or MDL with:
  - diffuse/base color from texture
  - roughness 0.9–1.0
  - metallic 0
  - very low specular
- Generate a tileable 1024x1024 or 2048x2048 dirt texture using layered noise:
  - base tan/brown soil color
  - low-frequency large blotches
  - medium-frequency sandy variation
  - sparse darker flecks/pebbles
  - subtle dry straw/gray patches
- Texture should tile over world space, e.g. repeat every 4 meters.
- Avoid strong high-contrast patterns.
- No displacement at first.
- Optional: generate matching normal map later.
